Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Introduction

GymSense ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. GymSense is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Camera Access: The app uses your device's camera solely for real-time pose detection. Video frames are processed entirely on your device and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Workout Data: Your exercise statistics (reps, sets, form scores) are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use GymSense.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to use. All AI processing happens on-device using TensorFlow Lite, meaning:

  • No video data leaves your device
  • No workout data is uploaded to any server
  • No analytics or tracking is performed

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Workout history and statistics
  • App preferences and settings
  • Achievement progress

This data can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

GymSense does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works completely offline.

Children's Privacy

GymSense does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
